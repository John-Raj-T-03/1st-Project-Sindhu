Managing a Community Radio initiative

I am currently working as the Station head and Content producer of 'Radio Monsoon' which is part of the project 'Forecasting with Fishers' by the University of Sussex. As the Station head, I am supervising the daily weather forecast dissemination process of our weather production team. I have to edit the forecast texts, check the audios and the overall actions on a day-to-day basis. I am also responsible for answering the queries of the fishermen and acting as a bridge between the official scientists who produce forecasts and the Fishers who are at the receiving end.
