I am a Co-Founding member of this prestigious organisation. Coastal Students Cultural Forum (CSCF) is a community based voluntary organisation registered under Travancore Literature and Scientific Society Act, 1955 since 2016. CSCF is a student and youth-led voluntary organisation, which is committed to safeguarding the interests of the marine fishing population in Kerala. It has been working with the coastal higher education students over the last four years and encouraging them to continue their higher education both qualitatively and quantitatively together with voluntary commitments to the coastal community progress and overall development.

Coastal Students Cultural Forum, a non-governmental organisation was formed for the empowerment of students and youth in the fishing community. It works among students of 42 fishing villages in Southern India. From an organisation that addressed the essential academic needs of students, CSCF has evolved as the voice of the fishing community over time. In 'Kadalkoodam', a platform set up by CSCF as part of its commitment to preserving the Indigenous Local Knowledge (ILK) as advised by UNESCO, elder fishermen will share the wealth of information about their occupation, the traditional language, culture and community living..
